A Little Bit About Me

Sadie Rawling's Bio

My name is Sadie Rawlings, and I am finishing up my freshman year at BYU. I grew up in Cumming, GA and miss the humidity more than anything, but I'm learning to love exploring Utah. Currently, I'm pursuing a major in the business school which has yet to be determined. One of my favorite parts about this semester was being able to go to an Olivia Rodrigo concert, camping, bonfires, and celebrating birthdays. However, I am very excited for the summer weather! I'm grateful for the opportunity to take IS 201 and learn all these new skills.
